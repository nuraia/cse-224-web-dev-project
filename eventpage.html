Begin Your Dream and Become a 3D Character Animator


Get your feet wet by learning animation basics in Autodesk Maya®, the most popular 3D animation software in the industry. We built our Maya Workshop for beginners so you can learn what you need to start your journey to becoming a successful character animator.

Interact with Maya from Day One


On day one, our instructors will take you through a step-by-step walkthrough of the interface. You'll also get weekly, one-hour Maya demos so you can continue to expand your understanding of how to navigate and animate within Maya.

Workshop Requirements

  • Have a good understanding of the English language.
  • Have intermediate computer software skills.
  • Meet the minimum computer system requirements.

Instructors Guide You to Succeed

Maya can be an intimidating interface. Our mentors are experts and will help you become comfortable with the Maya interface in 6 weekly, Live Q&As. They want to ensure that when you choose to start Animation Basics, all you have to do is focus on animating instead of struggling with the software.

Have Fun!

Once you create your first animation, pat yourself on the back—we knew you could do it. And remember to have fun!
